Wondering how to get your horoscope on Snapchat? It's pretty simple and you'll be judging your life decisions on star alignments in no time!

It's crazy to think that Snapchat started off as a messaging platform so thirsty folks could send each other saucy pics without the other individual holding onto said image forever. Alerting folks that an image was screen-captured further solidified that notion and now, several years since its release, it's still one of the most widely used messaging platforms in the world with a slew of added features, like horoscopes. But how do you get them on the app?

How do you get horoscopes on Snapchat?

If you've got the application, then it's pretty simple. Here's what you have to do:

  • Click on your Bitmoji icon in the app's upper left hand corner.
  • You're now in your profile; once you're there, click on the star sign underneath your username.
  • Once you do that, a prompt asking if you want to opt-in to the astrology portion will pop up on the screen.
  • You'll be asked to put in your birth date and area of birth.

After you do that, then your star chart will begin to appear before your very eyes, unlocking all the cosmic mysteries of the universe that pertain to your fate, which was decided the moment you were born because each individual human being is super important and not some coincidence spurred on by our mammalian need to procreate.

It goes beyond those little blurbs you find in a 250-cent booklet at the checkout counter in the supermarket, too.

Snapchat provides you with an "Astrological Profile" that delineates a lot of different characteristics that are entirely peculiar to you. It includes what your presiding "Elements" are, along with "Polarity" and "Modality." You get a grand total of 13 slides that delve deep into your astrological persona, which includes some interplanetary garble.

Like most horoscopes, everything is worded fairly vaguely and positively ambiguous so you feel like whatever's put on the page can directly apply to you.

Whatever your thoughts on astrological signs are, you have to give credit to whoever developed this aspect of the app. It looks really cool and the level of granularity that's afforded to users is super impressive.
