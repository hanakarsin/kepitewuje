15 Christmas Designs to Bring Holiday Cheer to Your 'Animal Crossing' Island

July 07, 2024
Post

It's the holiday season, and what would Christmas be without some holiday cheer on your 'Animal Crossing' island? Here's how to spruce up your island.

Whether you're new to Nintendo's Animal Crossing franchise or have been playing for years, you're probably familiar with the game's ability to incorporate real-life holidays into gameplay. Players have been able to celebrate everything from birthdays to engagements in Animal Crossing: New Horizons, and players could even throw a Thanksgiving dinner with their villagers with the game's Turkey Day event.

But ahead of the game's Toy Day event on Dec. 25, you might want to spruce up your island with some holiday cheer. Use these Christmas designs created by other players to really make your island feel like you're walking in a winter wonderland. We've got clothes, signs, and more to make sure your island is as festive as ever.

Rock a look that's not an ugly Christmas sweater.

Now, you can look like the reason for the season. We love an ugly Christmas sweater, but we don't need to wear them all the time. This one is the happy medium between the holiday season and something you can wear throughout the year.

Gather all your best singers.

Remember the days when carolers were singing down every block? Now you can help bring that holiday nostalgia back with this sign on your island. Get a bunch of friends together and sing along to K.K. It even has a book so you don't forget the lyrics.

Hang a classic wreath in your home.

Or on your simple panels. There are tons of wreaths and hangable options in the game, but at least this way you can choose a more traditional option that even gives you the choice of some festive candles.

Create a Christmas market.

Now that we have all these shopping items in the game, we might as well make a Christmas market! Put any items, DIYs, or baked goods up for sale but make sure you bake some cookies for Santa just in case he stops by.

Get an upgrade to Santa's coat.

Let's be honest ‚Äî Santa has been around for years and his look hasn't changed much at all. But that's where Animal Crossing comes in! Let this be your excuse to give him the makeover he deserves, since he does so much for us every year.

Spruce up your stalls with this holly design.

Is it really Christmas if you don't make any holiday-themed stalls around your island? Of course, this design can be used for other purposes, but the holly really solidifies the Christmas feel.

Make a snow-covered stall with this design.

With snow blanketing your island during the holiday season, you can add these stall designs for an extra snowy touch.

Or make a candy stall with this candy cane design.

If you still have candy left over from the Halloween events (or you have other custom designs that would work with this), this candy cane design would make a perfect stall on your island.

Use this Christmas quilt design wherever you feel necessary!

Whether on the ground as a blanket or on a cushion, this Christmas quilt design is bound to make your island feel like you really pulled out all of the Christmas decorations. 

This snowflake stone adds a subtle winter vibe to your island.

Since the background of this snowflake stone is transparent, you can add this stone to any stone paths you already have on your island for that extra holiday feeling.

Add these snowflakes to really sell the winter wonderland feel.

Even after the first snow has hit your island, you can make it feel even more like the holiday season by adding these snowflakes wherever you feel is necessary.

This ice would make a perfect ice rink.

Luckily, your island won't actually be covered in patches of slippery ice this winter, but this design is perfect for making an ice rink in a section of your island!

This cookie design adds to the holiday spirit.

Just because we can now cook in the game doesn't mean you have to skip out on additional food designs ‚Äî especially when it comes to what is arguably one of the best parts of the holiday season: cookies! Put this design on an outdoor table and it'll look like you're in the process of making yourself some cookies for Santa.

Make a tree farm and put this sign in front of it.

The game's pine trees make a perfect Christmas tree farm! While chopping down these trees won't actually give you one to put in your home, you can at least pretend and make yourself a little Christmas tree farm. 

And some festive Christmas sweater designs spread more holiday cheer.

It's not the holiday season without at least one tacky Christmas sweater. Thanks to this creator, you'll have a multitude of holiday sweaters and other apparel to wear (and display in your Able Sisters' shop so your villagers will wear them).
