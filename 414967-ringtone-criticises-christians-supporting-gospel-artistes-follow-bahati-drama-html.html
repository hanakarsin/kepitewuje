Controversial gospel singer Ringtone Apoko has answered criticised Christians for not supporting gospel music and musicians. He further said the majority only focus on keeping up with Bahati's drama.

Ringtone shared a video on his Instagram page, condemning Bahati for dissing him. He asked Kenyans to stop following what he was doing as he quit doing gospel ministry a long time ago.

"I would like to tell Christians to leave Bahati alone. He quit gospel music. I do not know why people are still stuck on Bahati and his ways. Let them follow the true artistes. I released a song but they are not watching. Let Bahati do his thing," he said.

More advice

The self-proclaimed chairman of gospel urged Christians to follow the real artistes singing true gospel music rather than keeping up with what the Mama singer does.

According to him, Bahati no longer serves God and that he is now doing greater sins that Christians would stop following up with.
