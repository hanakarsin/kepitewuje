Kikuyus will never love Raila Odinga, Moses Kuria tells Uhuru

Gatundu South MP Moses Kuria has told President Uhuru Kenyatta that Kikuyus will never love or accept his handshake partner Raila Odinga.

Kuria had accompanied Deputy President William Ruto on his tour to Gatanga Constituency, Murang'a county where several other Tanga Tanga politicians were present.

Speaking during a church service in the area on Sunday, February 28, Kuria said Kikuyus had already made up their minds that Ruto will be the next head of state after Uhuru finishes his second and final term.

"In as much as we respect you, we know that we elected you and not Raila. There is no day that we will ever love Raila," he said.

On Saturday, February 27, Kang'ata warned there were people who were planning to disrupt Ruto's meeting in the region.

He stated in politics, one should use persuasion and not violence to win the masses.

“Those plotting evil schemes should be ashamed of themselves. In politics one uses persuasion, not violence.”

In his tour, Ruto was accompanied by Tanga Tanga MPs such as Alice Wahome (Kandara), Ndindi Nyoro (Kiharu), Moses Kuria (Gatundu South) and Rigathi Gachagwa (Mathira) among others.

In a separate story, Ruto warmed the heart of a Murang'a woman after carrying and embracing her young daughter upon his arrival in the county.

The little girl had a bouquet of flowers to welcome the DP and upon handing them to him, she reached out to his shoulder and Ruto sweetly hugged her and carried her.
