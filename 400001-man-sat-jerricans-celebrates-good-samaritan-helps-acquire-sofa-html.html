A Twitter user by the name Burger plug @BrodricksKits shared a lovely story attaching previous posts that showed just how far he had come in life.

A post back in early December 2020 showed Brodricks' joy after purchasing a TV stand and how nicely he arranged his belongings inside it.

Come Wednesday, January 13, he had moved to a self-contained house and had even bought a nice TV set but was still sitting on water jerricans.

Just a day later, he shared a post showing a new set of seats that he purchased with the help of a good Samaritan who helped him pay for it.

He even acquired a house mat, grey in colour and slightly different shade from his L-shaped sofa that gave his house a really nice look.

He wrote:

"So this guy asks for my number, calls - asks how am planning to get a seat, I tell him I paid a deposit to pay the rest in three months. It has ended in premium tears of joy."

In a similar story, TUKO.co.ke recently reported about a humble bachelor who left many impressed with his simple and organised bedsitter.

A Facebook user named Innocent Criminol chose to be counted among those who are proud of their humble beginnings.

The bachelor, who lives in a mabati room, knows too well that he can make do with whatever little he has.

His walls are decorated with special nets and his floor is covered in a neatly placed PVC carpet.

The surrounding in his home is very organised. Going by the photos he shared on the Facebook page Home Beautiful, his shoes are placed in an orderly manner and a corner in his room has been transformed into a workable kitchen.

Innocent even has a tiny rack he uses to store his utensils and the other side of his residence has been turned into a very cosy and comfy bedroom.
