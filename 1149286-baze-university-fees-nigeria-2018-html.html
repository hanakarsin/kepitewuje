Baze University fees

Tuition fees at Baze University vary depending on the program you are interested in. Fees range from N1,125,000 to N3,500,000 per semester for most programs. This can be paid in two installments or through a multi-installment plan.

How to pay your Baze University school fees?

You can make your fee payment if you are a returning student or a new student. Follow the procedure below:

  • Open the Baze University payment portal.
  • Select your user type as "New Student" or "Returning Student".
  • For returning students, sign in using your student ID and email address.
  • For new students, sign in using your application ID and email address.
  • Once you have signed in, follow the prompts to pay your fee.

    How can I apply for admission to Baze University?

    To apply to join the university, follow these steps:

  • Visit the Baze University online portal for admissions.
  • Click on "Create Applicant Account".
  • Fill out the form by providing your name, email, phone number, and desired password.
  • Once done, click "Create Account".
  • Your account will be created, and you will be directed to the login page.
  • Log in using your email and password.
  • Click on "New Application" on the right corner of the page.
  • Select your application type and the desired course.
  • Continue to fill out the required details and make the required payments.
  • Once done, click "Submit" and print your submission letter.
