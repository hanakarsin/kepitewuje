Mr Eazi in deep thoughts as hit song fails to make up to N1.9m after 10 million streams

February 19, 2024
Post

- Singer Mr Eazi recently took to Twitter with a matter that seemed to have been giving him sleepless nights

- The music star disclosed that a song made less than N1.9m in revenue despite having up to 10 million streams

- Some fans were quick to offer some explanation to ease the singer’s worry

Popular Nigerian singer Mr Eazi recently took to his official page on micro blogging platform, Twitter, to open up about a matter that seemed to have been giving him great concern.

The singer in his post disclosed that he had been taking a look at one of his songs that racked up as much as 10 million streams on an undisclosed digital music streaming platform.

Mr Eazi’s concern was mainly in the fact that despite making so much streaming numbers, it did not translate in the revenue.
