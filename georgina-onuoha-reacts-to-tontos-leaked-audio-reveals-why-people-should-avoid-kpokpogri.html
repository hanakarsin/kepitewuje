Georgina Onuoha Reacts To Tonto's Leaked Audio, Reveals Why People Should Avoid Kpokpogri

August 13, 2024
Post

Ex Nollywood actress Georgina Onuoha has reacted to the recently leaked audio of Tonto Dikeh and former boyfriend, Prince Kpokpogri making the rounds on social media.

Onuoha had condemned the actions of Kpokpogri and warned others to be wary of him.

She said it was totally unacceptable to record a conversation that ought to be confidential when Tonto was feeling very vulnerable and unhappy.

She went ahead to state that Kpokpogri was a vile man that should be avoided as much as possible, Onuoha pointed out to women that they could having an intimate time with him and the next thing they would be on social media.

The former actress also warned men that they could have a business deal with him and he might decide to blackmail and betray them.

Concluding her post, Onuoha said she was not a fan of Tonto but she had proven to be a better human being.

Below reads her post:

“If tomorrow they call on all men you this thing, will count your two left legs and come to answer “present”? @kpokpogri, you are not a human being, let alone worthy of being called a man.

“You are vile, mean and despicable to record a woman in her most vulnerable state without her consent then share it on social media to make caricature of her and her pain.

“One thing I appreciate about life is karma. It will find you, and it will teach you hard lessons of life.

“You may have won this battle with this woman, someday you will lose a greater war, and just as you try to shame, the world will go agog celebrating your shame and downfall.

“You are no man; you are a coward. In any civilized world, you will be sued for invading her privacy. You are truly despicable .

“Women stay away from this animal. Tomorrow you might be having an intimate time with him and you will find yourself on social media.

“Men if you are doing business with this animal be weary, he will blackmail you and betray you. He has shown the world that he lacks character and integrity to subject his lover to such agony.

“I am not a fan of the woman you did this to, but she has proven herself to be a better human being than you. And I’m sure, every respectable man and woman will give her a hug because she did her dirty. You are a pig”
