Zahn McClarnon Bio, Twin Brother, Parents, Family

Zahn was born as Zahn Tokiya-ku McClarnon on October 24, 1966, in Denver, Colorado. His middle name translates to “the first to come,” while his first name, Zahn, is a tribute to his great-great-great-uncle, an artist who was an elder on the Standing Rock Indian Reservation. Zahn has a fraternal twin brother.
