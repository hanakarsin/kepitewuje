MTN customer care number

MTN is the largest telecommunications network in Nigeria. The company has the widest coverage in the country, and of course, such a big amount of customers requires qualitative customers service.

MTN customer care number Nigeria:

  • Dial 180 from an MTN line (Press 9 to go the main menu, press 6 for support, press 0 to speak with a customer service agent)
  • Call 08031000180 from other networks and +2348031000180 from abroad
  • Send email  on customercare@mtnnigeria.net
  • Go to the official website for a communication with service workers in a live chat

Glo customer care number

Glo is one of the leaders in mobile communication network in Nigeria. The network is known for their tariffs with the per second billing. Of course, the customer care service of the company can help any customer at any time if there is such a need. And now we are going to talk about Glo customer care service.

There are more than 28 million subscribers, and of course, they have some questions, they need some help or information about the network. It should be said that Glo has one of the biggest customer care line in Nigeria. The line works 24/7, so customers are welcomed at any time.

Just dial:

  • 121 - to talk with the customer agent 24/7, the number is free, and you can discuss there any question related to Glo network
  • 171 - so-called “Glo Hurry Help Line”. This is the number of fast help, you don’t need to wait if you call this number but it costs N20
  • 444 - This is a special number for the questions related to the internet connection. The call to this service is free
  • 500 - this line is created to give the information about latest offers, tariffs, codes, and services. It is also free
  • Send an email to customercare@gloworld.com
  • Or call +229-98-121-121 or +229-98-030-151
