You Messed Up Anambra Yet Opposing Obiano's Govt

April 26, 2024
Post

The Anambra State chapter of the All Progressives Grand Alliance, APGA, has slammed the Peoples Democratic Party, PDP, for faulting the government of Governor Willie Obiano.

Naija News reports that the Anambra State Publicity Secretary, PDP, Mr Nnamdi Nwangwu, had in a statement on Friday, alleged that Obiano’s administration had taken the state two decades backwards.

Reacting to Nwangwu’s statement, the National Coordinator, Media Warriors Forum of APGA Media Warriors Forum, Evang Chinedu Obigwe, said the PDP is playing irresponsible opposition.

According to him, the opposition party had messed up Anambra State while they were in power.

He urged the people of Anambra to ignore the criticisms of the PDP, adding that their “stock in trade is armchair criticism” which the people no longer reckon with.

He commended Governor Obiano’s administration, nothing that throughout his tenure the civil servants did not embark on any protest against his government and that is because of his good performance in the state.

Obigwe said protest was a daily routine for civil servants in the state when PDP was in power because of their anti-people policy.

He added: “When PDP was in power in Anambra State all that they did was the share of the state’s allocation in Abuja and political assassination was also on the rise at that time.

“Today if they want to travel to Abuja or Lagos, they will go to Umueri to use Anambra international cargo and passengers Airport but they are finding it difficult to commend Obiano for the airport project. They are also feigning ignorance of the community chose your project of Obiano’s administration which their communities are benefited.

“That community chose your project initiative of Obiano’s administration is a commendable initiative that took development in the state closer to the people and the PDP are shying away from mentioning it.

“In fact, our teachers went on strike under PDP administration watch for complete one year and no experience of bad governance is worst than that. So, the people should ignore what the PDP is saying because Ndi Anambra are happy with Governor Obiano as he did his best for them and another commendable thing he did for them is giving them Soludo.”
