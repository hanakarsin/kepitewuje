'They'll Kill You Like Abiola', Nnamdi Kanu Warns Southeast Governors

March 09, 2024
Post

The leader of the Indigenous People of Biafra (IPOB), Nnamdi Kanu, has warned Southeast governors that “they will be killed” like MKO Abiola if they continue to do nothing while “Fulani invaders are ravaging” Biafraland.

Naija News reports that the IPOB leader who is on exile made this comment on the micro-blogging site, Twitter, where he stressed that if the governors were warned that the governors will not get either the Vice Presidency or the Presidency if they were initially promised to get that.

Quoting Section 1 of the Land Use Act which states that “All land comprised in the territory of each State is hereby vested in the Governor of that State,” Kanu asked why any southern, especially southeast governor “feel powerless while Fulani mercenaries are occupying our forests?”

His words: “To all the Govs who do NOTHING while FULANI invaders are ravaging our land, hear this: If they promised you the Vice Presidency, you won’t get it. If they promised you the Presidency, you won’t get it. And if you try too hard, they will kill you like they killed Abiola,” he warned.
