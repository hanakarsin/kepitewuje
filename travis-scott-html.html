Travis Scott - Age, Family, Bio

April 08, 2024
Post

American rapper who has been signed to Epic Records and Kanye West's GOOD music production, Very G.O.O.D. Beats. He released his debut EP titled Owl Pharaoh in May 2013.

Before Fame

He went to Elkins High School before going to the University of Texas at San Antonio and later dropped out to start a music career. He signed his first major label with Epic Records in 2012.

Trivia

He has made numerous guest appearances on songs including on "I Get It" with Meek Mill in 2012, and on "Animal" with B.o.B. and T.I. in 2013. He launched the rap collective JackBoys in 2019. 

Family Life

He was born Jacques Berman Webster II and raised by his grandmother. In April 2017, he began dating Kylie Jenner. They had a daughter named Stormi in February 2018, then went on to separate in October 2019. He has a sister named Jordan Webster and a brother named Joshua. In August 2021 it was announced that Jenner and Scott were expecting their second child together. The couple welcomed a son named Wolf Webster on February 2nd, 2022.

Associated With

He collaborated with Big Sean on the song "10 2 10 (Remix)" in 2013.
