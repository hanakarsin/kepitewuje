The Miz - Age, Family, Bio

April 05, 2024
Post

Named Mike Mizanin but best known under the stage name The Miz, he has held the WWE Championship, the Intercontinental Championship, and the Tag Team Titles. He also competed on Bravo's Battle of the Network Reality Stars, finishing second, in 2004.  

Before Fame

He was captain of his high school's basketball and cross country team and participated on student government. 

Trivia

He was first introduced to the public on MTV's Real World and The Challenge in 2001. 

Family Life

He has a step-brother named Jimmy and a step-sister named Tonia. He married former WWE Diva Maryse Ouellet in February 2014. They welcomed a daughter named Monroe in March 2018. They had their second daughter, Madison, in September 2019. 

Associated With

He made a guest appearance as Mario on a 2012 episode of Psych alongside James Roday.
