BBNaija Nengi Appointed As SSA To Bayelsa State Governor

March 03, 2024
Post

Former housemate of Nigeria’s biggest reality show, Big Brother Naija, Rebecca Nengi Hampson has been appointed as a senior special assistant to Governor Douye Diri of Bayelsa State.

The development was confirmed by the 22-year-old reality TV star on her microblogging site account on Friday, November 6 while responding to congratulatory messages from fellow reality tv stars and fans.

A month ago, the winner of the Big Brother Naija lockdown edition, Laycon was appointed as the Youth Ambassador of Ogun State by Governor Dapo Abiodun.

Governor Dapo gifted the singer a three-bedroom bungalow and N5 million, Naija News recalls.
