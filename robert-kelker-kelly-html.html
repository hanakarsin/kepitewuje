Robert Kelker-Kelly - Age, Family, Bio

March 13, 2024
Post

Known best for portraying Bo Brady on NBC's Days of Our Lives during the early-to-mid 1990s, Kelker-Kelly is also famous for playing Sam Fowler on Another World. In 2002, he began a more than decade-long run as Stavros Cassadine on ABC's General Hospital.

Before Fame

While studying at Wichita Collegiate School, he starred in productions of the musicals Guys and Dolls, Camelot, and Oliver!

Trivia

He played the role of Trent in the 1996 film Dream for an Insomniac.

Family Life

He and his three sisters grew up in Wichita, Kansas as the children of Robert and Jonetta Kelker. After his first marriage -- to Linda Rattner -- ended in divorce, he married Miriam Parrish.

Associated With

Though Kelker-Kelly briefly played Days of Our Lives' Bo Brady, Peter Reckell, who portrayed the character from 1983 to 1987; 1990-1992; and 1995-2012, is best known for the role.
