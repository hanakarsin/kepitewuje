Nigerian actress, Aolat Ayonimofe, recently decided to celebrate her birthday in a special way. Rather than doing something big for herself, she decided to surprise her brother with a wonderful gift.

The movie star who is the big sister to fellow actor, Olohuniyo, decided to buy him a car on her own birthday.

In the post shared on social media, Aolat was seen with her brother who was blown away by the gift seeing as it wasn’t even his own birthday.

In the caption of the post, the actress explained that she decided to make her brother smile on her birthday. According to her, she was tired of seeing him jump bikes despite being a known figure.

She wrote:

“It’s my birthday but I made him smileI’m tired of him jumping bikes as a known face, I’m happy this is happening kindly help me celebrate my aburo .. Koni pa e o”
