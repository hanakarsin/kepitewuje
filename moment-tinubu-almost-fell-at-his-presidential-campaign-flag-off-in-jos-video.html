Moment Tinubu Almost Fell At His Presidential Campaign Flag-Off In Jos (Video)

June 30, 2024
Post

The All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu, has sparked mixed reactions online after missing his step at the campaign flag-off in Jos.

Naija News earlier reported that President Muhammadu Buhari led a host of APC stalwarts to Jos, the Plateau State capital, for the official kick-off of campaigns for Tinubu and his running mate, Kashim Shettima.

A video making the rounds online captured the moment Tinubu almost fell off at the campaign ground before he was assisted by security personnel walking behind him.

This has sparked reactions online with many Nigerians expressing concern for his health and calling on the need to step down from the presidential race.

Meanwhile, this is not the first time that Tinubu has made Nigerians question his health challenges.
