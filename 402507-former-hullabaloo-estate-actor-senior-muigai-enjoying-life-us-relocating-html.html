Many people usually want to lead a good life characterised with all the good things that come with it.

While there are many places in Africa where many can go to and have all the benefits, quite a number believe in flying abroad, where many claim it is the land of milk and honey.

Whatever reason, whichever place, all that matter is the fact that many who try the change usually become successful and seem to be enjoying their new lives.

Former Hullabaloo Estate actor Senior Mungai recently brought the aforementioned to life with photos of him enjoying his life in the US.

Several photos of the star seen by TUKO.co.ke on social media are perfect proof America loves our boy.
